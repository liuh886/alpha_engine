### 4. `created_ts` Timestamp Format
The backend exclusively emits **seconds**. In `src/assistant/model_registry_index.py`, the backend assigns `now = time.time()`, returning seconds since the epoch. The frontend `model-normalizer.ts` anticipates this by parsing `new Date(row.created_ts * 1000)` to convert the seconds into milliseconds.
